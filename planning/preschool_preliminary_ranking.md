This ranking reflects your latest constraints:
- Home base fixed to Pekayon Jaya address.
- Continuity to Elementary and assumed 30% discount are noted only and excluded from scoring.
- All comparisons use three cost views: Monthly Cost, Year-1 Cost, and Year-2 Cost.

## Practical decision framing for capex and opex
- If priority is strongest academics regardless cost pressure: follow Quality Rank.
- If priority is cash-flow safety and capex control: follow Cost Rank.
- If priority is balanced education and affordability: follow Optimized Rank.

## Important note
Continuity assumption (same-school progression usually gets seat and discount of around 30%) is documented but intentionally excluded from rating scores and cost scores.
